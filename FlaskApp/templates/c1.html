Africanized Honey Bee

Dangers They Pose: Gang Up and attack humans if provoked

Insecticide: DeltaDust


Preferred Habitats: Africanized honey bees prefer nesting in a variety of locations, including trees, utility poles, buildings, and even underground cavities.

Lifecycle: Like other honey bees, Africanized honey bees undergo complete metamorphosis, consisting of egg, larva, pupa, and adult stages. The lifecycle duration can vary based on factors such as temperature and food availability, but typically ranges from 21 to 35 days.

Season They Are Active: Africanized honey bees are active year-round in warm climates, but they may be more aggressive during the warmer months when their colonies are at their peak.
